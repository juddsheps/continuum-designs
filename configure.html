The Glass — A Different Standard

Not a film.
Infused into the glass itself.

Most infinity tables use a thin reflective film adhered to standard glass. We tested over 10 Australian suppliers before rejecting them all. The results were shallow, inconsistent, and unconvincing.

Our glass is sourced internationally — made through a specialised process that infuses reflectivity directly into the material. The result is a clarity and depth that no film can replicate. The illusion doesn't just look deeper — it is deeper.

This is the reason the table has generated over 100 million views across TikTok and Instagram, and fulfilled hundreds of orders globally. People see the difference immediately.
